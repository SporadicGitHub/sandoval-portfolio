About Me

Hi, I'm Shunne! I'm a dedicated intern seeking opportunities to blend my technical know-how with my true passion for design. With a background in graphics and game design, I've contributed to projects by creating initial design mockups and translating them into functional applications. Although I've developed some skills in front-end development and software programming, my real strength lies in character creation and environment design. I've worked on projects from an e-commerce website to an accounting system where I not only implemented UI/UX designs using tools like Angular and Java Swing but also honed my abilities in design software such as Clip Studio Paint, Krita, Autodesk Maya, and Adobe Photoshop. I'm eager to join a collaborative team where I can continue to grow professionally while focusing on the creative aspects that drive my work.
